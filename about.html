About us

People first, because hospitality is a service industry

adarkenigma LLC helps independent and privately owned hotels get more from the team they already have. We are not here to sell you a platform or a machine. We are here to make your people better at the work that guests actually feel.

Why we don't upsell software

Plenty of vendors will hand you another dashboard. Few will sit at your front desk during a busy arrival or shadow a night auditor through a messy close. A warm welcome, an accurate folio, and a clean handover come from trained people, not from another subscription. So that is where we put the work.

How we work

We start by watching how your property really runs, then coach housekeeping, front desk, and night-audit staff on the habits that move your numbers. We leave routines your team can keep using long after we are gone, and we back the case with your own data rather than someone else's promises.
